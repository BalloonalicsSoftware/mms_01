Who we are

Since COVID-19, many events-related businesses were falling behind and unable to keep up and adapt to the changes that everyone was facing.
The founders of Matilda Marketing Services saw this as an opportunity to create a solution. MMS was created with two core things in mind: connecting the events industry with digital solutions.

MMS is a one-stop solution for indie B2C brands and small businesses with a focus on branding, social media marketing, e-commerce, and event based marketing.
