Who we are

Matilda Marketing Services (MMS) is more than just a marketing agency that gets things done. We are a boutique style marketing firm with a team of passionate professionals who bring their knowledge and enthusiasm into their work to help our clients succeed. We are dedicated to delivering personalized services and solutions to every brand and personality we work with.

As a boutique marketing agency, we prioritize building lasting relationships with our clients and understanding their unique needs and objectives. This approach enables us to provide tailored solutions that can help our clients establish a polished digital presence, build brand awareness, and generate new leads and sales. Our packages offer a range of services that includes digital brand positioning, in-person and online events marketing, front end website design, and social media management.

One of our focus is in empowering “legacy business” to succeed in today’s competitive marketplace and help them adapt to the online marketplace to attract new leads and retain existing customers with digital community management. Besides legacy business, we also help foreign brands localize by translating and creating copies that resonate with local Vancouver communities throughout different social media platforms. Whether you are a legacy business owner, a start-up entrepreneur/solopreneur, or a foreign brand new to YVR, Matilda Marketing Services team is here to guide you through every step of the process.
